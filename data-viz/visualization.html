Data Visualization Example

I selected this bubble chart because it turns all cyber events into an interesting story.

By sizing each bubble according to the number of records lost and color-coding them by the type of entity (like tech, healthcare, or government), it allows the viewer to immediately grasp the most important information.

The viewer can clearly understand which sectors are most vulnerable. He also notices really easily how the frequency of massive breaches has accelerated over the last decade, since the bubbles are getting bigger and more frequent over time.

This is also an interactive visualization following the correct process: overview first → zoom and filter → details on demand.
