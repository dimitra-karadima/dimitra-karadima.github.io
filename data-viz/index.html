About Me

I’m a software engineer with five years of professional experience, currently working in Data Science–focused teams. My work sits at the intersection of backend engineering and applied machine learning, where I design and build production systems that make data-driven models usable, scalable, and reliable.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked primarily with Python, building and maintaining APIs and services that support ML workflows and experimentation. My role has required more than just writing code — it involves thinking carefully about system design, reliability, observability, and how research-grade models transition into real-world systems. I’m particularly interested in the engineering challenges that arise in MLOps environments: reproducibility, deployment strategies, infrastructure constraints, and long-term maintainability.

Academically, I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Computer Science at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. I hold a Bachelor’s degree from the Athens University of Economics and Business, Department of Science and Technology. My academic path complements my professional work by strengthening my theoretical foundations in computer science, algorithms, and systems.

You can learn more about my work and interests through GitHub and LinkedIn.
